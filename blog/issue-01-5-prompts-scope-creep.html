2) RAID Extractor (Markdown tables)

Prompt
From the text, extract four tables: Risks, Actions, Issues, Decisions.
Columns:
- Risks: Risk | Trigger phrase (verbatim) | Prob (L/M/H) | Impact (L/M/H) | Mitigation | Owner
- Actions: Owner | Action | Due date (best guess) | Priority (H/M/L)
- Issues: Issue | Severity | Unblocker | ETA
- Decisions: Decision | Date | Context | Next review date
Return Markdown tables only.
