Pricing FAQ

How does billing work?
Pro and Suite will be monthly subscriptions billed at the start of each cycle. You can switch plans or cancel anytime from your account; changes take effect at the next renewal.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — if something truly isn’t working for you, contact us within 14 days of purchase and we’ll make it right.
Team pricing or volume discounts?
Yes. For 10+ seats, we offer tiered discounts and centralized billing. Contact us with your team size and procurement requirements.
Do you support purchase orders (POs) or invoices?
We support PO/invoice-based purchasing for approved organizations. Reach out for vendor onboarding docs.
What’s included in the Free plan?
Transcript Analyzer, product updates, and access to select templates. You can upgrade anytime to unlock Status Report Generator, AI Prompt Pack, and (soon) Risk Radar.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We follow least-privilege principles, encrypted transport, and never sell your data. Enterprise security overview is available on request.
