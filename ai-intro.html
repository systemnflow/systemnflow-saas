Copy‑paste starter prompts

1) Meeting → Actions/Risks/Decisions

You are a project analyst. From the transcript below, return:
- Actions (assignee, due date if mentioned)
- Risks (severity, mitigation)
- Decisions (owner, date)
Transcript:
[Paste transcript here]

2) Risk scan on tasks/issues

You are a risk analyst. From the task list, identify top 5 risks with mitigation.
Format: Risk | Why it matters | Likelihood | Severity | Mitigation | Owner | Due

3) Exec status one‑pager

You are a PM creating an executive summary. Produce a one‑page status with:
- Green/Yellow/Red
- Highlights
- Blockers
- Next 3 priorities
- Budget/Schedule note (if relevant)
Context: [Paste key notes]
