Privacy Policy

We collect as little as possible. We don’t sell your data. And we’ll never spam you.

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. What We Collect

When you submit a form (newsletter, contact, etc.), we collect the info you enter — like your email address. We also use anonymous analytics to track basic site usage (like which pages get visited).

2. Why We Collect It

To deliver product updates, respond to messages, and improve your experience. That's it. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Period.

3. Services We Use

We use trusted third-party tools:

  • FormSubmit – for handling form submissions
  • Google Analytics – for anonymous usage tracking

Their usage is governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

We only keep your data as long as necessary — usually to deliver an email or analyze site usage. Nothing more unless legally required.

5. Your Rights

You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send. Want your data deleted or corrected? Just email us at info@systemnflow.com.

6. Contact Us

Questions? Concerns? Suggestions? Hit us up at info@systemnflow.com.
