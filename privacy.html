Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need. We don’t sell your data. And we’ll never spam you.

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. What We Collect

When you fill out a form (like a contact form or newsletter signup), we collect the info you provide — like your name and email. We also use anonymous analytics to understand how people use the site (like what pages are visited).

2. Why We Collect It

We use this data to respond to messages, improve your experience, and deliver updates when requested. We never sell, rent, or share your personal information.

3. Services We Use

We work with a few third-party services:

  • FormSubmit – for handling form submissions
  • Google Analytics – for tracking basic anonymous usage data

These tools have their own privacy policies. We chose them because they keep things simple and safe.

4. Data Retention

We keep your info only as long as needed — usually just to respond to your message or track site usage. We delete data when it’s no longer relevant or required.

5. Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time. Want to update or delete your info? Just reach out to info@systemnflow.com and we’ll take care of it.

6. Contact Us

Have questions about your privacy? Contact us at info@systemnflow.com.
